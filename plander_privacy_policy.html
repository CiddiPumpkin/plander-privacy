Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2025
App Name: Plander

1. 📌 Summary

Plander does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud, and is never sent to our servers — because we don’t have any.

However, Plander uses Google AdMob for in-app advertisements, which may collect certain device information. Please read below for details.

2. 🔐 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not ask you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not store your data on any external server.
  • We do not track your activity within the app.

3. 📊 Information That MAY Be Collected (via AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain information automatically for analytics and advertising purposes, including:

  • Device model
  • OS version
  • App usage statistics (e.g., session duration, interactions)
  • Advertising ID
  • Country or region (approximate)

These data are collected by Google and are subject to their own privacy policy.
Google’s Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:
iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking

4. 💾 Data Storage

All app data (such as memos, preferences, schedules) is stored either:

  • Locally on your device, or
  • Securely within your iCloud account, if iCloud sync is enabled.

At no point is this data transmitted to our servers — because we don’t operate any.

5. 🧾 GDPR & Global Compliance

We comply with major data protection laws such as:

  • GDPR (EU)
  • CCPA (California)
  • PIPA (Korea)

Since Plander does not process personal data directly, your rights under these laws — such as access, correction, deletion — are inherently respected by design.

You may still exercise any of these rights by contacting us if needed.

7. 🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future. Any changes will be notified in-app or updated on this screen.
